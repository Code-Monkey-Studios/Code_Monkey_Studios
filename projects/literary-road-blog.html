Literary Road Blog {

Hi and welcome to the Literary Road project. The intent of this project, which you can see is ongoing, was to develop a blog to showcase the progress of the writing talent here at Code Monkey Studios and to also gives a sneake peak into what we are working on. The project has since evolved into a resource site with an exergesis for each story, plus a glossary of writing terms. There are various activities scattered amongst the post for aspiring authors to try their had at also.
